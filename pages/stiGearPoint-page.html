STI GearPoint: Kiosk Machine System

About

The STI GearPoint: Kiosk Machine System is a proposed solution to streamline the purchasing process for school uniforms and proware at STI College Davao. This self-service kiosk aims to address challenges such as long queues, limited service hours, and inconvenient manual processes by offering features like an intuitive touchscreen interface, direct payment integration, and receipt generation for order tracking. By automating these tasks, the system enhances efficiency, reduces human error, and improves the overall experience for students and staff, reflecting the institution's commitment to technological innovation.
